Cereal

Description

Cereal and milk for breakfast is a familiar, quick, and adaptable choice that combines the crunchiness of cereal with the creaminess of milk. It's a staple breakfast for many and can be tailored to individual tastes and dietary preferences.

Ingredients

  • Cereal
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Get Bowl
  2. Pour milk into bowl
  3. Add cereal to milk
