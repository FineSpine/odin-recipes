Poha

Description

Poha is made from pressed rice grains. It is a light breakfast. It ha a mildly sweet and mainly salty taste but the specifics depend on the region where it is being consumed. It is best served with chopped onions, sev, chopped tomatoes and a squeeze of lemon on top.

Ingredients

  • Poha
  • Water
  • Condiments
  • Potatoes
  • Dressing

Steps

  1. Steam poha
  2. Mix poha with condiments and sides of choice
  3. Garnish poha with dressings
