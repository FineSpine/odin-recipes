Sandwich

Description

A sandwich is a popular and versatile food item typically consisting of one or more fillings placed between slices of bread. The choice of ingredients and condiments can vary widely, allowing for a vast array of sandwich types and flavors.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Condiments
  • Potatoes

Steps

  1. Get Bread
  2. Place fillings on one bread
  3. Cover fillings with other bread
